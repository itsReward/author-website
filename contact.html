Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept speaking engagements?

Yes, I'm available for speaking engagements including literary festivals, university lectures, book clubs, and writing workshops. Please use the contact form above with details about your event, and my team will get back to you promptly.

How can I arrange a book club discussion with you?

I love connecting with book clubs! Please send me details about your book club, including the number of members, location, and preferred dates at least 2 months in advance. Virtual discussions can also be arranged.

Do you offer writing mentorship?

I periodically offer mentorship to emerging African writers. These opportunities are announced on my social media and through my newsletter. I also teach at the Lagos Literary Workshop several times a year.

Can I send you my manuscript for review?

Due to time constraints and legal considerations, I'm unable to read unsolicited manuscripts. I recommend seeking feedback from writing groups or professional editors, and submitting to literary agents when your work is ready.

How can I request a signed copy of your books?

Signed copies can be requested through my publisher or at my in-person events. You can also send a copy of the book with a self-addressed, stamped envelope to my literary agency address, and I'll sign and return it when possible.

Do you have any upcoming events?

All my upcoming events are listed on my social media profiles and sent to newsletter subscribers. Consider subscribing to stay informed about readings, signings, workshops, and festival appearances.
